About the laboratory

A holistic search for the next generation of therapeutics.

AMNPR is a leading laboratory under the Centre for Research Excellence, dedicated to the scientific study of medicinal plants and natural products beyond conventional medicine. We meet the urgent need for new, reliable therapeutic agents — and establish their mechanisms with rigorous proof.

Through an integrated approach spanning biochemistry, molecular biology and simulation, we pursue alternative medication systems for the non-communicable diseases rising fastest across South Asia.

01 Phytochemical discovery

Isolation, characterisation and bioactivity screening of plant-derived compounds.

02 Molecular mechanisms

Pathway-level dissection of how natural products modulate disease targets.

03 Computer-aided design

Molecular docking, network pharmacology and in-silico ADMET profiling.

04 Translational models

In vivo validation across oxidative-stress and metabolic disease models.

Research themes

Where plants meet precision biology.

Exceptional research needs cutting-edge tools. Our facilities enable innovative explorations across six connected themes.

Medicinal plants

Screening indigenous and ethnobotanical species for bioactive fractions with therapeutic potential.

Molecular pharmacology

Elucidating signalling pathways — oxidative stress, apoptosis, inflammation — at the cellular level.

Computational biology

Docking, network pharmacology and CADD pipelines that prioritise candidates before the bench.

Functional foods

Evaluating nutraceuticals and functional foods that strengthen immune and metabolic health.

Non-communicable diseases

Targeting diabetes, cardiovascular, neurodegenerative and renal disorders with natural products.

In vivo validation

Preclinical disease models confirming efficacy, safety and serum-biomarker responses.
